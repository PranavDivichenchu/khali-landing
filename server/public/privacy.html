Khali

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

Summary: Khali does not sell your data, does not require account creation, and collects only minimal, anonymous usage data to improve the app experience.

1. Introduction

Khali ("we", "our", or "the app") is an AI-powered news aggregation application that delivers curated, multi-perspective news stories. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using Khali, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • AI Chat Questions: When you ask a question about a news story using the in-app AI feature, your question text is sent to our server and processed using OpenAI's API. We do not permanently store your questions or associate them with your identity.
  • Voting: When you vote on political perspectives (left/right), the vote is recorded anonymously for aggregate statistics. No personally identifiable information is attached to your vote.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifier: We generate a random, anonymous device identifier to track aggregate app usage (e.g., daily active users). This is not linked to your Apple ID, name, email, or any personal information.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous, aggregated usage data including: stories viewed, categories browsed, time spent in the app, and feature interactions (e.g., tapping "Reveal Claim"). This data is used solely to improve the app.
  • Crash Data: If the app crashes, Apple may automatically collect and share crash logs with us through App Store Connect. These may contain technical device information but no personal data.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any contact information.
  • We do not require account creation or login.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or location.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited data we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • App Improvement: Understanding which features and categories are popular helps us improve the app.
  • AI Responses: Your questions are processed in real-time to generate AI-powered answers. They are not stored after the response is delivered.
  • Aggregate Statistics: Vote counts and interaction data are aggregated (never individual) to provide features like consensus indicators.

4. Third-Party Services

Khali uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Powers AI-generated summaries, podcast audio, and question-answering. Your questions are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. OpenAI's privacy policy applies to their handling of this data: openai.com/privacy.
  • Supabase: Our backend database and storage provider. All data is stored securely. supabase.com/privacy.
  • Pexels: Provides stock background media. No user data is shared with Pexels.

We do not use any advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or analytics services that track users across apps (no Facebook SDK, Google Analytics, AdMob, etc.).

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit).
  • Server data is hosted on secure, reputable cloud infrastructure.
  • We retain anonymous usage analytics for up to 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted.
  • We do not store AI chat conversations beyond the immediate request-response cycle.

6. Children's Privacy

Khali is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information or require accounts, there is minimal data associated with any individual user. However, you have the right to:

  • Request Information: Ask us what data, if any, is associated with your device identifier.
  • Request Deletion: Ask us to delete any data associated with your device identifier.
  • Opt Out: You can stop using the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes all locally cached data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify users through an in-app notice or by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
